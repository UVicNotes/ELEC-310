## Unilateral Z-Transform

_definition_: We define the __Unilateral Z-transform__ as,

$$\begin{aligned}
    \tilde{X} (z) = \sum_{n = 0}^{\infty} x[n]z^{-n}
\end{aligned}$$

**e.g.**

*Consider,*

$$\begin{aligned}
    y[n] - 5y[n-1] + 6y[n-2] = 3x[n-1] + 5x[n-2]
\end{aligned}$$

*given $x[n] = (0.5)^n u[n]$ what is y[n]?*
